📈 Periodic Trends — Animated Graph

Select any property to see how it changes across all 54 elements (Period 1–5). These are the exact trends from Class 11 NCERT Chapter 3.

→ Across a Period (Left to Right)
Atomic radius ↓ — more protons pull electrons closer.

Ionization energy ↑ — electrons harder to remove.

Electronegativity ↑ — stronger pull on bonding electrons.

Metallic character ↓ — elements become less metallic.
↓ Down a Group (Top to Bottom)
Atomic radius ↑ — more electron shells added.

Ionization energy ↓ — outer electrons farther, easier to remove.

Electronegativity ↓ — less pull on bonding electrons.

Metallic character ↑ — elements become more metallic.
